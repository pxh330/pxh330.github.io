Why Xiaohan built this?

If you by any chance see this website, let me give you a big welcome first:). Welcome to Xiaohan's World!

I'm Xiaohan, an engineering undergrad who is not very engineering. In my 2nd year of uni, I realized social media gradually became the only way people expressing themselves completely. You put basic info about you on facebook, some memorable moments on instagram, your education and profession on LinkedIn… Everything is formatted and I’m just so tired of using the same layout and wondering if I can make a website of my own, then I can put whatever I want to display on it, my 'amateur aesthetic stuffs', fun techy stuffs… everything.

So, here I am. I learned basic html, css, javascript, some front-end frameworks and now as you can see, I’m gradually setting everything up. It’s not easy to learn something from scratch, but I found myself changing and gradually falling in love with what I’m doing along the way. At first my aim was to build a website, but I ended up obtaining front-end knowledge and eventually stepped into the fascinating world of computer science.

I didn’t intend to tell a story, but I really want to tell YOU, who has read all of these this far:

Don't be afraid to put on your adventure hat when you see something attractive. Just try it, commit to it, and see how far you can go. People tend to forget about their dreams nowadays, and some even don’t think they have one. I’m here to encourage you to bravely try your thoughts, which you might be reluctant to do at first. During your adventure, it's very likely that you may encounter something not quite like the way you thought before, or totally new to you…... But trust me, keep trying then you will eventually find what you want, and you will get there.

Thank you sooooo much for reading all the words above, now please feel free to start your journey in my space. Have a good time :)!
